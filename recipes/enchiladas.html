Enchiladas Suizas

Description

Chicken enchiladas with a roasted tomaltillo cream sauce.

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 6 medium sized tomaltillos
  • 3 anaheim chilis
  • 2 yellow chilis
  • 1-3 serano chilis, depending on desired spice
  • 1 yellow onion
  • Cumin
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Ciliantro
  • Crema
  • Lime Juice

Enchiladas

  • Chicken
  • Taco Spices
  • .5 yellow onions
  • 1 bell pepper
  • Cheese
  • 1 can of rotel
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees farenheit.
  2. Poach chicken till easily shredded with a fork, cool and set aside (alternatively you can use this dip as filling).
  3. Slice peppers, tomaltillos and onions, roast in a single layer on a baking sheet until peppers are blistered and slightly charred.
  4. While your chicken and roasting veggies are doing their thing, chop up half an onion and 1 bell pepper into small pieces.
  5. Shred you chicken and mix with your chopped veggies, 1 can of rotel(drained of most liquid) and your favorite taco spice mix. Don't measure, just let the spices speak to you.
  6. Shred some cheese up in that chicken mixture.
  7. Now for the sauce. Take those roasted veggies and put them in a big old blender. If you only have a small blender, puree it in batches. Get it nice and smooth. Add in some garlic, cumin and other spices, lime juice and that whole bundle of fresh cilantro and blend it up.
  8. Now you'll add in the crema. Start with a cup and keep adding till you like the consistency. Taste it and add whatever spices you think it needs.
  9. Roll your chicken mixture into torillas, lay out in a greased casserole pan. Cover evenly with the sauce and then cover with a liberal amount of shredded cheese.
  10. Lower the oven's temperature to 375, and bake for 20-25 minutes untill top is golden brown and sauce is bubbling.
